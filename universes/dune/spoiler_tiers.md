# Spoiler Tiers: Dune

## arrakis_arrival — Arrival on Arrakis
House Atreides arrives on Arrakis. Paul encounters the desert for the first time. The spice. The sandworms. The Fremen as rumor and shadow. The political trap is visible but not yet sprung. Covers the first portion of the novel and Dune: Part One (2021).

## fremen — Among the Fremen
Paul and Jessica survive the betrayal and reach the Fremen. Stilgar. Sietch Tabr. Chani. Paul begins his education in Fremen ways. His prescient visions sharpen. The messianic machinery of the Missionaria Protectiva begins to work. Covers the middle section of the novel and the opening of Dune: Part Two (2024).

## muadib — Muad'Dib Rises
Paul has fully become Muad'Dib. He has passed the water of life. His prescience is near-total. The Fremen are ready. The jihad is visible on the horizon. Covers the lead-up to the final confrontation.

## dune_complete — Dune Complete
The first book is finished or both films have been seen. Paul has defeated the Emperor. The jihad has begun. Paul rules. The ending of the story is known — and its terrible implications understood.

## messiah — Dune Messiah
Twelve years after Paul's victory. The jihad has killed billions. Paul navigates a conspiracy of enemies — the Bene Gesserit, the Spacing Guild, the Tleilaxu, and Princess Irulan — who seek to depose him. Hayt arrives. Paul's vision of the future contracts. His fate.

## everything — Everything
Children of Dune and beyond. Leto II's transformation. The Golden Path fully revealed. The deepest layers of what Herbert built. No limits on spoilers.
