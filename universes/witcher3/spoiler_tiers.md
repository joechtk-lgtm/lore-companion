# Spoiler Tiers: The Witcher 3

Each tier is cumulative — a user at `skellige` tier can receive all content from `white_orchard` and `velen_novigrad` as well.

---

## Tier: white_orchard
**Safe to reveal:** Everything up to leaving White Orchard.
- Geralt's background, general witcher lore
- The fact that Geralt is looking for Yennefer
- Basic world state: war between Nilfgaard and the North
- Ciri exists and Geralt cares about her (no details on why she's being hunted)
- Nilfgaard occupying parts of the North
- Vesemir's existence

**Not safe:** Ciri's Elder Blood, the Wild Hunt's specific goals, Yennefer's location, anything from later acts.

---

## Tier: velen_novigrad
**Safe to reveal:** Everything through Velen and Novigrad acts.
- Ciri has been in Velen — visited the Crones, escaped, moved on
- The Bloody Baron's full story (his wife, his daughter, the deal with the Crones)
- Ciri's time with the Crones and what she did to escape (burned the forest)
- Ciri was in Novigrad, was being hunted by the Eternal Fire and witch hunters
- Philippa Eilhart is in Novigrad, in hiding
- The Lodge of Sorceresses is scattered after the Conclave
- Whoreson Junior's fate
- Radovid's role as a tyrant persecuting mages
- Dandelion was captured
- General Ciri history: her Nilfgaard connection, her Elder Blood (can be referenced)

**Not safe:** Ciri's arrival in Skellige, her vision of the White Frost, events in Skellige, Kaer Morhen battle, ending details.

---

## Tier: skellige
**Safe to reveal:** Everything through the Skellige arc.
- Ciri was in Skellige, nearly died on the ice, was rescued by Crach's clan
- Uma's curse and what Uma actually is (Avallac'h's doing)
- Avallac'h's role as Ciri's guide/captor
- The Wild Hunt wants Ciri for her Elder Blood to stop the White Frost
- Ciri's vision: the White Frost is coming, she may be the only one who can stop it
- The Wild Hunt's composition: Eredin, Caranthir, Imlerith
- Imlerith killed Vesemir (NOT safe — this is kaer_morhen tier, see below)

**Not safe:** The Kaer Morhen battle specifics, Vesemir's death, the final battle, any ending.

---

## Tier: kaer_morhen
**Safe to reveal:** Everything through the Kaer Morhen battle.
- The Wild Hunt attacked Kaer Morhen
- Vesemir died protecting Ciri — she unleashed her full power in grief
- Ciri is captured by the Wild Hunt after Kaer Morhen (or fled, depending on player choices)
- Ciri's full power has been revealed
- The Lodge's involvement in the final plan

**Not safe:** Final confrontation with Eredin, which ending the player gets, the nature of the White Frost solution, post-game fates.

---

## Tier: main_complete
**Safe to reveal:** Everything in the main game.
- All three possible endings for Ciri (witcher, empress, dead)
- Eredin's defeat and death
- The nature of the White Frost
- Radovid's assassination (if player chose that path)
- Nilfgaard's victory in the war (or peace, depending on path)
- All main storyline character fates

**Not safe:** Hearts of Stone and Blood and Wine DLC events and characters.

---

## Tier: everything
**Safe to reveal:** All content including both DLCs.
- Hearts of Stone: Gaunter O'Dimm (Master Mirror), Olgierd von Everec, the deal and its resolution
- Blood and Wine: Toussaint, Anna Henrietta, the Unseen Elder, Syanna, the final fate of Regis
- Geralt's vineyard (Corvo Bianco) and potential retirement
- All DLC character arcs and fates
