# Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Volcano Manor
Volcano Manor sits in the slopes of Mt. Gelmir, west of Altus Plateau. [game_eldenring] It operates as a guild — visitors are welcomed and offered contracts. The contracts ask for assassination. The lord of the manor is not seen at first.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## Rykard
Rykard is a demigod — son of Radagon and Rennala. [game_eldenring] He resented the Golden Order and sought to subvert it. His method: he fed himself to the God-Devouring Serpent within Mt. Gelmir. As the serpent consumed Rykard, Rykard consumed the serpent — merging with it, gaining power outside the Golden Order's control. He now exists as the serpent, speaking with a human voice from within it. He seeks to devour all the gods.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## The Lord of Blasphemy
As Lord of Blasphemy, Rykard reigns from deep within Volcano Manor. [game_eldenring] His followers lure other demigods' champions to be killed — feeding their power to Rykard. He holds a Great Rune. His voice, from within the serpent, is still recognizably human: sardonic, contemptuous of divinity. He calls the Tarnished "maggot."
