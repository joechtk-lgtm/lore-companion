# Miquella the Unalloyed

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Miquella is an Empyrean and twin of Malenia. [game_eldenring] He was born with a curse of his own: he cannot age past childhood. He has the body and apparent age of a child, but the mind of someone far older. He crafted unalloyed gold — a material that resists outer god influence — to try to free his twin from the Scarlet Rot. He also used it to create an alternative to the Erdtree's grace.

## The Haligtree
Miquella grew the Haligtree as a refuge from the Erdtree. [game_eldenring] It was meant for those the Erdtree rejected: Omens, those afflicted with rot, those outside the Golden Order's grace. He believed he could grow it into a new god-tree. He put himself into a cocoon of unalloyed gold inside the Haligtree to try to age — to become a full god through natural growth.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Taken by Mohg
Mohg, Lord of Blood invaded the Haligtree and took the cocoon — with Miquella inside. [game_eldenring] He installed it in Mohgwyn Palace and devoted himself to it. Miquella did not respond.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## The Land of Shadow
Miquella was not dormant in the cocoon — he was already gone. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] He shed his outer self, leaving his arm behind, and crossed into the Land of Shadow to become a god. To do so, he stripped himself of everything that made him himself: his compassion, his love, his attachments. He became something colder and more absolute. In the Land of Shadow, the Tarnished — his "beloved" — follows him and must ultimately confront what he has become.
