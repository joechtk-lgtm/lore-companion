# Queen Marika the Eternal

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Marika the Eternal is the god of the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] She is the vessel of the Elden Ring, chosen by the Greater Will. Immortal, all-powerful, and sovereign over the Golden Order. She was the wife of Godfrey, the First Elden Lord, and later consort of Radagon. She bore demigod children who became the shardbearers after the Shattering.

She shattered the Elden Ring following an event that broke her faith. This caused the Shattering — the great war among her demigod children.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Radagon
Radagon of the Golden Order was her second consort. He was first married to Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon, before Marika summoned him to become her Elden Lord. The full truth of their relationship is stranger still. [game_eldenring]

## The Sealed Erdtree
After the Shattering, the Erdtree sealed itself. Marika's fate within it is unknown to the Tarnished approaching Leyndell. [game_eldenring]

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Radagon Is Marika
Radagon and Marika are the same being — two identities, one existence. [game_eldenring] Radagon spent his existence trying to repair what Marika broke. When the Tarnished reaches the Erdtree's heart, they fight Radagon, who labors still to fix the Elden Ring. Marika was imprisoned within the Erdtree as punishment for shattering it — her golden form suspended within the thorns.

[spoiler_tier: main_complete]
## After the Endings
In most endings, the Tarnished becomes the new Elden Lord and either restores or remakes the Elden Ring. Marika's shattered body is the instrument through which the new order is established. [game_eldenring]

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Origins in the Land of Shadow
Marika was not always a god. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] She was born in the Badlands — the Land of Shadow — and was once a follower of the Crucible, the primordial force of life that predated the Erdtree. She rose to become the Greater Will's champion, conquered the Lands Between, and supplanted the old order with the Golden Order. The ruins and shadow trees of the Land of Shadow preserve the history she left behind.
