# Malenia, Blade of Miquella

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Malenia is a demigod and twin of Miquella. [game_eldenring] She is widely regarded as the deadliest warrior in the Lands Between. She was born cursed — afflicted by the Scarlet Rot, an outer god's mark. She suppresses the rot through will alone.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## The Battle of Aeonia
Malenia and Starscourge Radahn met in battle at Aeonia in what is now Caelid. [game_eldenring] Their clash ended in mutual defeat. To stop Radahn, Malenia released a bloom of Scarlet Rot — the rot spread across Caelid, transforming it into a blighted wasteland. Both warriors fell. Malenia retreated into dormancy somewhere in the north.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## The Haligtree
Malenia is dormant in the Miquella's Haligtree — a great tree Miquella grew as a refuge from the Erdtree, home to the forsaken. [game_eldenring] She waits there in her consecrated snowfield. She has been waiting a long time.

[spoiler_tier: main_complete]
## The Goddess of Rot
When the Tarnished fights Malenia, she has already begun to transform. [game_eldenring] In her second phase, she becomes the Goddess of Rot — the outer god's mark finally flowering. She no longer fully suppresses it. She fights still for Miquella, but Miquella is gone.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Miquella's Beloved
In the Land of Shadow, Malenia's devotion to Miquella — and why she chose to serve him rather than rule herself — becomes clearer. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] She is not simply his guardian. She chose him. The Scarlet Rot she carried, the battles she fought, the Haligtree she protected — all for him.
