# Radagon of the Golden Order

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Radagon was the second Elden Lord, consort of Queen Marika the Eternal. [game_eldenring] A red-haired champion of the Golden Order, he devoted himself to perfecting and repairing the Elden Ring. He fathered demigod children: Ranni and Rykard with Rennala; Malenia and Miquella with Marika.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## Marriage to Rennala
Before becoming Marika's consort, Radagon was married to Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon, head of the Academy of Raya Lucaria. [game_eldenring] Their union was political — a peace treaty between the Golden Order and the Academy. When Marika summoned him, he left Rennala behind. She has never recovered. The Academy's scholars remain loyal to Rennala's grief.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## The Truth: Radagon Is Marika
Radagon and Marika are not two people. They are the same being, a single existence with two aspects and two names. [game_eldenring] Marika shattered the Elden Ring. Radagon spent his entire existence trying to repair it. When the Tarnished fights the final boss of the Erdtree, they fight Radagon — still struggling to fix what the other half broke. He falls. The Elden Beast emerges from the Elden Ring.
