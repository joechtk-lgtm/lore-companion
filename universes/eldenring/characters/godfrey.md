# Godfrey, First Elden Lord

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Godfrey was the first Elden Lord — the first to hold the title, married to Queen Marika and wielding the power of the Elden Ring through his Great Rune. [game_eldenring] Originally Hoarah Loux, a great warrior chieftain of the Badlands, he conquered the Lands Between alongside Marika. He is the distant ancestor of Godrick the Grafted.

After the wars were won, Marika stripped him of his grace and exiled him. He and his warriors were the first Tarnished — cast out into the outer lands to continue warring until grace would call them back.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Return to Leyndell
Godfrey answers the call when the Elden Ring shatters. [game_eldenring] He returns to Leyndell and waits before the Erdtree. He greets the Tarnished not as an enemy but as a worthy opponent — the first challenge worthy of his attention in an age. He fights as Godfrey, First Elden Lord.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Hoarah Loux
Beneath the Elden Lord, Godfrey is still Hoarah Loux the warrior. [game_eldenring] When Godfrey falls, Hoarah Loux emerges — he abandons the Elden Lord's power and fights with his bare hands. He was a warrior before he was a lord. He is still a warrior. He is among the final battles before the Erdtree.
