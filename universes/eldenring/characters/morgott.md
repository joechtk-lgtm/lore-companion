# Morgott, the Omen King

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Margit the Fell Omen
A figure called Margit the Fell Omen bars the path to Stormveil Castle. [game_eldenring] He warns the Tarnished away from the Erdtree, calling their ambition foolish. He wields a golden sword of light alongside a cane. He is not what he appears to be.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Morgott
Margit is Morgott — Omen King of Leyndell, the Last of All Kings. [game_eldenring] He is a son of Marika and Godfrey, born an Omen: cursed with horns, claws, and the rot of the outer world. Omen children were thrown into the Erdtree's roots at birth — into the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds beneath Leyndell. Morgott escaped. He hid his nature, took the guise of Margit, and has spent his existence warning off those who would disturb the Erdtree. He guards Leyndell as its last king, despite the Erdtree rejecting him at birth.

## His Words
When the Tarnished defeats Morgott in Leyndell, he calls them "Cursed one" and weeps. [game_eldenring] He guarded a grace he was denied his entire life. He is one of the most sympathetic figures in the Lands Between.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## After Leyndell
After Morgott's death and the burning of the Erdtree, Leyndell becomes Leyndell, Ashen Capital. [game_eldenring] Morgott's corpse remains where it fell. The city he protected is gone.
