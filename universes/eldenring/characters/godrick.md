# Godrick the Grafted

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Godrick the Grafted rules Stormveil Castle in Limgrave. [game_eldenring] He is a descendant of Godfrey the First Elden Lord, but carries none of his ancestor's greatness. When the Shattering began, Godrick fled — he hid in Stormveil rather than fight the other demigods.

## Grafting
Godrick grafts the limbs of defeated warriors onto his body to absorb their power. [game_eldenring] His form is a mass of extra arms, some still writhing. In battle, he grafts a dragon's head onto his stump to breathe fire. He is described as the "most cowardly" of the demigods — he steals power rather than earning it.

## His Great Rune
Godrick holds the Great Rune of the Grafted — a shard of the shattered Elden Ring. [game_eldenring] He is the first shardbearer most Tarnished face.
