# Mohg, Lord of Blood

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Mohg is a demigod — twin of Morgott, son of Marika and Godfrey. [game_eldenring] Like Morgott, he was born an Omen and thrown into the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds beneath Leyndell. Unlike Morgott, he did not emerge to protect the Erdtree. He heard another voice instead.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Mohgwyn Palace
Mohg heard the whispers of the Formless Mother — an outer god of blood. [game_eldenring] She granted him blood flame and a path forward. He escaped his prison and established Mohgwyn Palace: a vast underground realm soaked in blood, outside the normal geography of the Lands Between. He leads the Mohgwyn Dynasty from there.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Miquella's Cocoon
Mohg kidnapped the cocoon of Miquella from the Haligtree. [game_eldenring] He placed the cocoon on an altar in his palace and devoted himself to it — pouring blood upon it, singing devotions, waiting for Miquella to wake and become his consort. He sought to create a new dynasty blessed by the outer god of blood. Miquella remained dormant. Unresponsive. The cocoon never opened for him.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Miquella's Departure
Miquella never woke for Mohg because Miquella was already gone. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] He shed his outer self — left his arm behind in the cocoon — and passed into the Land of Shadow. The cocoon the Tarnished finds in Mohg's palace contains only what Miquella left behind.
