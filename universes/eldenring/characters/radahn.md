# General Radahn, Starscourge

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Radahn is a demigod — son of Radagon and Rennala, making him kin to both Leyndell and the Academy. [game_eldenring] He mastered gravitational magic, which he used to hold the stars in place. He loved his horse, Leonard, and used his gravitational power to carry his own great weight — he grew too large for a normal horse to bear. He is one of the mightiest demigods.

He went mad after his battle with Malenia. The Radahn Festival is held at Redmane Castle in Caelid — warriors gather to fight him and end his suffering.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## The Battle and Caelid
Radahn and Malenia fought at Aeonia. [game_eldenring] Malenia's Scarlet Rot bloom ended the battle. The rot transformed Caelid. Radahn fell but did not die — he wanders the blighted fields of Caelid in madness, eating the corpses of those who came to fight him. His Great Rune remains with him.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Why He Held the Stars
Radahn held the stars in place to prevent a specific star from falling. [game_eldenring] That star was tied to Ranni's plan. When Radahn is defeated, the star falls — it lands on the Siofra River underground, opening the way to Nokron, Eternal City. This is what Ranni needed. She engineered the Radahn Festival indirectly by ensuring someone would need what the star's fall would reveal.
