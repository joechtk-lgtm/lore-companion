# Melina

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Melina appears to the Tarnished at Sites of Grace. [game_eldenring] She calls herself "a finger maiden who has lost her finger" — she has no master, no purpose given to her. She offers to become the Tarnished's maiden: burning the runes they gather into power (leveling up). She asks for nothing in return except to be taken to the foot of the Erdtree.

She carries a closed eye — her right eye is always shut.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Kindling
To open the Erdtree and reach the Elden Ring, something must burn. [game_eldenring] Normal fire does not touch the Erdtree. Melina offers herself as the kindling — her body to start the flame at the Forge of the Giants. She calls herself "born of her [Marika's] flesh, yet harboring no Erdtree grace." She will sacrifice herself if asked.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## The Forge
At the Forge of the Giants, Melina burns. [game_eldenring] She asks the Tarnished to "abandon your commitment to the Erdtree" and set fire to the thorns. She disappears after this, her purpose fulfilled or exhausted.

[spoiler_tier: main_complete]
## The Frenzied Flame Ending
If the Tarnished accepts the Frenzied Flame before the ending, Melina's closed eye opens — it shows the flame of frenzy. [game_eldenring] She appears one final time and says she will hunt the Tarnished down. She vows to kill the Lord of Frenzied Flame before the flame can spread. Her eye was closed to hide this: the power to end what the Three Fingers would begin.
