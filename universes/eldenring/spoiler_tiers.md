# Spoiler Tiers: Elden Ring

## limgrave
Just started. First steps in the Lands Between. Includes: Stormveil Castle, Godrick the Grafted, basic world overview. Margit the Fell Omen encountered but not identified. Ranni appears but her full story unknown.

## liurnia
Past Stormveil. Raya Lucaria and the Academy. Radahn Festival in Caelid. Ranni's questline beginning. The state of Caelid (aftermath of Malenia vs Radahn). Mohgwyn Palace accessible but Mohg's true story not yet revealed.

## altus
Altus Plateau and approach to Leyndell. Morgott the Omen King revealed (he was Margit). Volcano Manor and Rykard's true nature. Leyndell, Royal Capital. The need to burn the Erdtree established. Godfrey returns to Leyndell.

## mountaintops
Mountaintops of the Giants, Consecrated Snowfield, Crumbling Farum Azula. Maliketh defeated. Radagon IS Marika — confirmed. Marika imprisoned in the Erdtree. Night of Black Knives full story: Ranni's role, Godwyn's fate. Mohg's design with Miquella's cocoon. The burning of the Erdtree. Godfrey / Hoarah Loux final form.

## main_complete
Any ending achieved. Age of Stars (Ranni), Elden Lord (standard), Lord of Frenzied Flame, Age of Fracture, Age of Duskborn, Blessing of Despair. Melina's forbidden eye and her vow. Full scope of all endings.

## everything
Shadow of the Erdtree DLC complete. Miquella's departure from the cocoon. The Land of Shadow — Marika's origins. Miquella's sacrifice of his emotions. The final confrontation in the Land of Shadow. All DLC characters, locations, and lore.
