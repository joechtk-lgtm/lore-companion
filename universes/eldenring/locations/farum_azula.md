# Crumbling Farum Azula

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Existence
Crumbling Farum Azula is an ancient city suspended in the sky, amid a perpetual storm. [game_eldenring] It exists outside the normal flow of time — its ruins are frozen in the moment of their destruction, always crumbling, never fully fallen. Beastmen and ancient dragon-kin still inhabit it.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Before the Golden Order
Farum Azula predates the Golden Order, the Erdtree, and perhaps the Lands Between as it exists now. [game_eldenring] It was a place of ancient power — the Fell God and Maliketh's origins are connected to it. Beast Clergyman (Gurranq) is found there in his true form.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## The Burning
To reach Farum Azula, the Tarnished uses the Forge of the Giants — the act of burning the Erdtree transports them there. [game_eldenring] Within Farum Azula, Maliketh, Black Blade waits. Maliketh holds the Rune of Death — the fragment locked away by Marika, the rune stolen by Ranni's assassins. When the Tarnished defeats Maliketh, the Rune of Death is released, and Leyndell begins to fill with ash. Farum Azula then truly begins to fall.
