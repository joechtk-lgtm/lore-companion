# Leyndell, Royal Capital

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Leyndell is the seat of the Golden Order — the royal capital of the Lands Between, built at the base of the Erdtree. [game_eldenring] It is vast: a sprawling city of towers, plazas, and fortifications. The Tarnished must defeat two shardbearers before approaching it.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## The Capital
Leyndell was built around the carcass of an ancient dragon — the streets wind around its petrified bones. [game_eldenring] The city is enormous and layered: outer walls, a colosseum, the royal palace, and the Erdtree itself rising from the center. Morgott guards its throne. The Golden Order's clergy have in many cases deteriorated — those who remained too close to the sealed Erdtree became "Those Who Live in Death" or simply went mad.

A hidden city exists below: the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, where Omens were thrown and the Three Fingers wait.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Leyndell, Ashen Capital
After the Erdtree burns, Leyndell is covered in ash. [game_eldenring] The golden city becomes grey. The Erdtree's frame remains — charred, enormous, still structurally present. The city is now called Leyndell, Ashen Capital. Some areas become accessible that weren't before. Godfrey's shade waits here.
