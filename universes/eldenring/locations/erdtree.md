# The Erdtree

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Erdtree is the immense golden tree that dominates the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] It is visible from everywhere — a vast radiant presence at the center of the world. It is the physical manifestation of the Golden Order's power and the symbol of the Greater Will's presence.

The Erdtree governs rebirth in the Lands Between: those who die return to it, are purified, and are reborn. Grace comes from it. Sites of Grace draw power from it. After death, the Tarnished revive at Sites of Grace rather than returning to the Erdtree — they are outside its usual cycle.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## The Sealed Tree
After the Shattering, the Erdtree sealed itself. [game_eldenring] No one can enter. No grace comes from it. The thorns that grew around it cannot be burned by normal fire. The Golden Order persists, but its source has gone quiet. Within Leyndell, the Erdtree's physical roots and branches permeate the city — it has grown into and through the capital.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## What Burns It
Only very specific kindling can set the Erdtree alight. [game_eldenring] Melina offers her body as that kindling. The Frenzied Flame can also serve. At the Forge of the Giants — the ruins where the giants once fed the Erdtree-destroying flame — the burning can be accomplished. The Erdtree burns, Leyndell becomes ash, and the path to the Elden Ring within opens.
