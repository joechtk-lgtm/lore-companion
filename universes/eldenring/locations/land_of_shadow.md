# The Land of Shadow

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Overview
The Land of Shadow is the setting of Shadow of the Erdtree. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] It exists hidden in the shadow of the Erdtree — a realm beneath or behind the Lands Between, accessible through the dried cocoon Miquella left behind in Mohgwyn Palace.

## What It Contains
The Land of Shadow holds ancient ruins, shadow trees (mockeries or predecessors of the Erdtree), and the remnants of a history Marika erased. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] This is where Marika was born, where she came from before she became the god of the Lands Between. The ruins preserve the culture she left behind and the followers she abandoned.

## Miquella's Path
Miquella passed through here to become a god. [dlc_shadowoftherdtree] The Tarnished follows his footsteps. Along the way, they encounter Miquella's "beloved" — those he charmed into devotion — and learn what Miquella sacrificed to reach his goal. He stripped himself of his compassion. He is not the figure his followers believed him to be.
