# The Shattering

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Shattering was the great war among Marika's demigod children that devastated the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] It began when Queen Marika shattered the Elden Ring — the event that preceded the call to the Tarnished. The shards of the ring became Great Runes. The demigods seized them and went to war.

The war ended without resolution. No demigod won. Each retreated to their domain with their Great Rune, and the Lands Between settled into ruin. The Golden Order persists in form but not in power. The Tarnished arrive after this.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## The Battle of Aeonia
The most decisive and destructive battle of the Shattering was between Malenia, Blade of Miquella and Starscourge Radahn. [game_eldenring] They fought in Aeonia, in what is now Caelid. Neither could defeat the other by normal means. Malenia unleashed a bloom of Scarlet Rot. The rot spread across Caelid, transforming it permanently into a blighted wasteland. Both warriors fell. The battle ended Caelid as a livable region.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Why Marika Shattered It
Marika shattered the Elden Ring deliberately, after the Night of Black Knives broke her. [game_eldenring] The death of Godwyn — her first son, beloved by all — and what it revealed about the Greater Will's design shattered her faith. Marika and Radagon are the same being: Radagon tried to repair the ring even as Marika broke it. Their existence was a war within a single body.
