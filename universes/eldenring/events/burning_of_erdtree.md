# The Burning of the Erdtree

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## What Must Be Done
The Erdtree sealed itself after the Shattering. [game_eldenring] It cannot be entered. The Elden Ring is within. To reach it, the Erdtree must be opened by fire — but no ordinary fire touches it. The thorns around it are impervious. Something specific must serve as kindling.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## The Forge and the Kindling
The Forge of the Giants is ancient — the giants once used it to feed a flame that could threaten the Erdtree. [game_eldenring] It is now dormant, waiting atop the Mountaintops of the Giants. Melina offers herself as the kindling: her body, burned at the Forge, will set the Erdtree alight. This is her purpose — or the purpose she has chosen. Alternatively, the Tarnished can carry the Frenzied Flame as their own kindling, sparing Melina but committing to a different ending entirely.

Melina burns. The Erdtree ignites. At that moment the Tarnished is transported to Crumbling Farum Azula.

## Leyndell After
When the Tarnished returns from Farum Azula — after defeating Maliketh and releasing the Rune of Death — Leyndell is already ash. [game_eldenring] The Erdtree's golden exterior has burned away. The frame remains, charred and enormous. The city that was Leyndell is now Leyndell, Ashen Capital. The Elden Ring is now accessible within the Erdtree's heart.
