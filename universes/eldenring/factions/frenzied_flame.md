# The Frenzied Flame

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Frenzied Flame is a force opposing the Erdtree and the Greater Will. [game_eldenring] It represents the dissolution of all order — boundaries between life and death, self and other, all burned away. Its followers claim it is liberation. Its enemies call it ruin.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## The Three Fingers
The Three Fingers communicate the Frenzied Flame's will from beneath Leyndell, in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds. [game_eldenring] To reach them, the Tarnished must descend through the underground of the city and strip off all armor and possessions at the final door. The Three Fingers will then embrace the Tarnished, marking them as the Lord of Frenzied Flame.

Hyetta is a follower — she appears across Liurnia, guided by a vision of the flame, eating the "grapes" that are human eyes.

[spoiler_tier: main_complete]
## The Ending
The Lord of Frenzied Flame ending: the Tarnished ignites the Flame of Frenzy and becomes a vessel of chaos, burning the Elden Ring and the Erdtree to ash. [game_eldenring] All order dissolves. Melina, if she survived, appears at the end: her forbidden eye open, vowing to hunt the Tarnished down.
