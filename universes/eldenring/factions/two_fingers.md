# The Two Fingers

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Two Fingers are physical manifestations of the Greater Will's communication — pairs of giant white fingers housed in sacred locations. [game_eldenring] They are oracles: they receive the will of the outer god and relay it through their Finger Readers. There is a set of Two Fingers for each Empyrean candidate.

The Two Fingers in the Roundtable Hold guide the Tarnished: mend the Elden Ring, become Elden Lord.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## Finger Maidens and Silence
Each set of Two Fingers is tended by a Finger Maiden. [game_eldenring] Melina calls herself "a finger maiden who has lost her finger" — she has no Two Fingers to serve, no instructions. Many of the Two Fingers fell silent or contradicted each other after the Shattering.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## The Three Fingers
The Three Fingers are a different entity entirely — an opposing force. [game_eldenring] Where the Two Fingers communicate the Greater Will's order, the Three Fingers offer the Frenzied Flame: chaos, dissolution, the burning of all boundaries. They are hidden deep beneath Leyndell in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds. To reach them, everything must be abandoned.
