# The Golden Order

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Golden Order is the ruling religion and power structure of the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] It was founded by Queen Marika after she conquered the Lands Between and established the Erdtree as the supreme object of faith. The Order holds that the Erdtree's grace governs all life and death: those who die return to the Erdtree, are purified, and are reborn. Those outside the Order's grace are denied rebirth.

The Golden Order supplanted older powers — particularly the Crucible, the primordial force of life that predated the Erdtree, and the ancient dynasties (dragons, giants) that the Order's armies destroyed.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Structure
The Order is enforced by the Two Fingers (oracles of the Greater Will), the Erdtree clergy, the Knights of Leyndell, and the Erdtree Avatars that patrol the land. [game_eldenring] Leyndell is its seat of power. After the Shattering, the Order persists in hollow form — Leyndell still stands, its knights still patrol, but the Erdtree has sealed itself and the clergy have in many cases gone mad.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## What the Order Suppressed
The Golden Order was built on suppression. [game_eldenring] The Crucible — the primordial force of mingled life — was declared heresy. The Rune of Death was locked away in Maliketh so that the Erdtree could control who died and who was reborn. The old gods were destroyed or subjugated. The Order's grace was a cage as much as a gift.
