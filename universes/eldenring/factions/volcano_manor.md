# Volcano Manor

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Volcano Manor is a sprawling estate built into Mt. Gelmir, west of Altus Plateau. [game_eldenring] Tanith runs it in the name of its lord, who is not seen at first. Visitors are welcomed. An air of something wrong hangs over the manor.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## The Guild
Volcano Manor operates as an assassination guild. [game_eldenring] Guests are eventually given contracts: written requests to invade and kill specific targets — champions and envoys of demigods, primarily. The targets are chosen for a reason: they feed into something larger.

Joining the manor as a questline offers rewards and eventually leads to confrontation with Rykard himself.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## Rykard's Design
Rykard — the serpent-god lord of the manor — uses Volcano Manor as a feeding mechanism. [game_eldenring] The deaths of champions feed his growing power. He seeks to devour all gods, starting from within the mountain. Tanith serves him with absolute devotion that is uncomfortable to witness.
