# The Tarnished

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Tarnished are those who lost the grace of the Erdtree and were exiled from the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] Godfrey was the first: after the wars were won, Marika stripped him of grace and sent him and his warriors into the outer lands. They became wanderers beyond the Erdtree's sight.

When the Elden Ring shattered, grace called the Tarnished back. Only the Tarnished can still perceive grace — those in the Lands Between lost it when the Erdtree sealed itself. The Tarnished are guided by grace toward the shards of the Elden Ring, toward becoming Elden Lord.

## Why the Tarnished
The Tarnished cannot be easily killed — they revive at Sites of Grace. [game_eldenring] They are outside the Erdtree's usual control of life and death. This makes them uniquely suited to the task of gathering Great Runes and mending or remaking the Elden Ring.
