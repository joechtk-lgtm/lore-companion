# The Greater Will

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Greater Will is an outer god — a cosmic entity from beyond the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] It sent the Elden Ring to the Lands Between and chose Marika as its vessel. It communicates through the Two Fingers and governs the Golden Order's doctrine. Its name is spoken with reverence by the clergy.

Its nature and motivations are deliberately obscure. It does not speak directly. Its intentions must be inferred from the Two Fingers' guidance.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Is It Good?
The Greater Will's order required the suppression of the Rune of Death, the destruction of older powers, and the imprisonment of those who resisted. [game_eldenring] It is a cosmic force, not a benevolent parent. Ranni rejected it explicitly — she saw the age it built as a cage. Whether the Tarnished serves it, overthrows it, or replaces it determines which ending they reach.
