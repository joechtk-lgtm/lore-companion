# The Outer Gods

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Outer Gods are cosmic entities whose influence reaches into the Lands Between from beyond it. [game_eldenring] They empower followers, mark vessels, and advance their own incomprehensible agendas. The Greater Will is an outer god. It is not the only one.

[spoiler_tier: liurnia]
## Known Outer Gods
- **The Greater Will**: Sent the Elden Ring. Governs order. Communicates through the Two Fingers. [game_eldenring]
- **The Formless Mother**: Outer god of blood. Patron of Mohg. Her name is whispered, not spoken. [game_eldenring]
- **The Outer God of Scarlet Rot**: Marks Malenia. Seeks to spread the rot across the Lands Between. Has no known name. [game_eldenring]
- **The Frenzied Flame / Fell God**: Chaos and dissolution. Communicates through the Three Fingers. [game_eldenring]
- **The Moon**: Connected to Ranni's path after she killed her original patron. Ranni becomes its vessel by the Age of Stars ending. [game_eldenring]

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Their Conflict
The outer gods do not coordinate. They compete. [game_eldenring] The Lands Between is a site of their conflict — each attempting to install their preferred order (or disorder). Marika served the Greater Will and destroyed its rivals. The Shattering disrupted that dominance. Now all of them reach in.
