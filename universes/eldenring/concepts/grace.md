# Grace

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
Grace is the golden light given by the Erdtree — the divine favor that guided those in the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] It appeared as golden wisps showing the way. Sites of Grace are places where grace is strongest — resting points along the path where the Tarnished can heal, level up, and attune magic.

After the Shattering, those in the Lands Between lost grace. They cannot see it. The Tarnished — exiled and stripped of grace long ago — can still perceive it. This is why grace called the Tarnished back.

## Guidance
Grace wisps appear in the world, pointing toward the Erdtree or toward major objectives. [game_eldenring] They are guidance without explanation. They show a direction, not a reason.
