# The Great Runes

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Great Runes are the shards of the shattered Elden Ring, each held by one of Marika's demigod children. [game_eldenring] Each Great Rune imbues its holder with immense power — but also with obsession, madness, or corruption. The runes are the source of the Shattering's conflict.

## Known Runes
- Godrick holds the Great Rune of the Grafted [game_eldenring]
- Rennala holds the Great Rune of the Unborn
- Radahn holds a Great Rune
- Morgott holds the Great Rune of the Omen King
- Rykard holds a Great Rune
- Mohg holds the Great Rune of the Unborn (corrupted by the Formless Mother)
- Malenia holds the Great Rune of the Unalloyed

To use a Great Rune, it must first be restored at its corresponding Divine Tower.

[spoiler_tier: altus]
## The Rune of Death
A special case: the Rune of Death — or a fragment of it — was locked away in Maliketh by Marika. [game_eldenring] The Erdtree's control over life and death depended on this: without the Rune of Death, true death was impossible. Those who died returned to the Erdtree. This was by design. Ranni stole a fragment of it on the Night of Black Knives.
