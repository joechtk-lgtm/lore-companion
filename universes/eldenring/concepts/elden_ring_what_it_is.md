# The Elden Ring

[spoiler_tier: limgrave]
## Overview
The Elden Ring is the highest of all laws — the fundamental governing principle of the Lands Between. [game_eldenring] It was placed here by the Greater Will. It resides within the Erdtree. Its power flows through Marika, who is its vessel.

The Elden Ring is composed of the Great Runes — shards of divine law. Together they form the ring. Shattered, they are individual powers distributed among the demigods.

To become Elden Lord is to mend or remake the Elden Ring and inherit its power.

[spoiler_tier: mountaintops]
## Its True Form
The Elden Ring is not merely a law or an artifact. [game_eldenring] It is a living thing — or the vessel of one. The Elden Beast is the physical form of the Greater Will's order, the living expression of the Elden Ring itself. When the Tarnished fights their way to the heart of the Erdtree, they fight Radagon (trying to repair the ring) and then the Elden Beast (the ring itself, defending its existence).
