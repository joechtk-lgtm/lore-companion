# The War of the Ring

## Overview

The War of the Ring is the defining conflict of the Third Age — the final confrontation between Sauron and the Free Peoples of Middle-earth over the One Ring, which Sauron had lost in the Second Age and needed to recover his full power. It is fought on multiple fronts simultaneously: the siege of Minas Tirith, the assault on Rohan, the campaign in Ithilien, the battle at the Black Gate — and the quiet, parallel quest of two hobbits across Mordor, which is the war's actual hinge.

## Timeline

**Year 3018 Third Age**
- Gandalf identifies the Ring in the Shire. Frodo leaves Bag End.
- The Nazgûl hunt the Shire and the road south.
- The hobbits reach Bree, meet Aragorn, cross to Rivendell.
- The Council of Elrond. The Fellowship is formed. [book_lotr]

**Year 3019**
- The Fellowship attempts the Pass of Caradhras; they turn to Moria.
- Gandalf falls with the Balrog on the Bridge of Khazad-dûm.
- The Fellowship reaches Lothlórien; they depart with gifts from Galadriel.
- Boromir tries to take the Ring from Frodo at Amon Hen; Frodo and Sam take the boats and go east alone; the Fellowship is broken.
- Boromir dies defending Merry and Pippin from Uruk-hai; Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli pursue. [book_lotr] [film_fellowship]

**Rohan and Minas Tirith (3019)**
- Merry and Pippin escape into Fangorn; the Ents rouse and attack Isengard.
- Gandalf returns (as Gandalf the White); Théoden is restored; the Battle of Helm's Deep.
- Frodo and Sam, guided by Gollum, reach Cirith Ungol; Shelob; Sam takes the Ring; Frodo is captured.
- Sam rescues Frodo from the Tower of Cirith Ungol; they march toward Mount Doom.
- The siege of Minas Tirith; the Battle of the Pelennor Fields; Théoden falls; Éowyn slays the Witch-king.
- Aragorn takes the Paths of the Dead and arrives with the dead army.
- The army of the West marches to the Black Gate as a diversion.
- Frodo reaches Mount Doom; Gollum takes the Ring and falls into the fire; the Ring is destroyed; Sauron falls. [book_lotr] [film_two_towers] [film_return_king]

## The Resolution

The war ends with the Ring's destruction and Sauron's permanent defeat. Aragorn is crowned King Elessar. The Reunited Kingdom is established. The Elvish age ends; the Elves sail West; the Fellowship disbands. Frodo, unable to heal fully from the wound of the Morgul blade and the weight of the Ring, eventually sails West with the Ring-bearers. Sam, Merry, and Pippin grow old in the Shire. Sam is eventually permitted to sail West as the last Ring-bearer. [book_lotr]
