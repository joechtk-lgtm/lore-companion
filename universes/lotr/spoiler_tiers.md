# Spoiler Tiers: Lord of the Rings

## shire — The Shire
The story begins. Bilbo's birthday party. Gandalf's return. The Ring passed to Frodo. The Black Riders arrive. Frodo and companions leave the Shire. Covers the opening of The Fellowship of the Ring up to the departure from Hobbiton and the early journey.

## rivendell — Rivendell + Moria
The Fellowship reaches Rivendell. The Council of Elrond. The Fellowship formed. The crossing of Caradhras. The Mines of Moria — Khazad-dûm and its darkness. Covers from Rivendell through Moria and the emerging of the surviving Fellowship at the eastern gates.

## rohan — Rohan + Helm's Deep
The Fellowship is broken at Amon Hen. The Three Hunters. Merry and Pippin and the Ents. Rohan, Edoras, Théoden's awakening. The battle of Helm's Deep. Covers The Two Towers through the end of the battle at Helm's Deep and Isengard's fall.

## gondor — Gondor + Mount Doom
The siege of Gondor. The Pelennor Fields. The Black Gate. Sam and Frodo in Mordor. Mount Doom. The end of the War of the Ring. Covers the conclusion of The Return of the King through the destruction of the Ring and its immediate aftermath.

## lotr_complete — Trilogy Complete
The full trilogy — all three books or all three films — is complete. The Grey Havens. The Scouring of the Shire (books). The departure of the Ring-bearers. Everything through the endings of the story. No limits within the primary trilogy.

## everything — Everything
The Silmarillion, The Hobbit, Rings of Power, extended canon. The First Age, the Second Age, the deep history. Númenor, the forging of the Rings, the Ainulindalë, the full scope of Tolkien's legendarium. No limits on spoilers from any source.
