# Sith

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]

The Sith are the ancient enemies of the Jedi, practitioners of the dark side of the Force who embrace passion, ambition, and the pursuit of power. [film_ot] Where the Jedi seek serenity and selflessness, the Sith code teaches that peace is a lie — that through passion one gains strength, through strength power, through power victory, and through victory the chains of limitation are broken. [film_ot] During the Galactic Civil War, the Sith are represented by Emperor Palpatine (Darth Sidious) and his apprentice Darth Vader, who together rule the galaxy through fear and the dark side. [film_ot] Vader ultimately turns against his master to save his son Luke, killing the Emperor and fulfilling the prophecy of the Chosen One who would bring balance to the Force — though at the cost of his own life. [film_ot]

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]

For a millennium before the fall of the Republic, the Sith operated in secrecy under the Rule of Two: one master to embody power, one apprentice to crave it. [film_pt] This doctrine was established by Darth Bane, who recognized that the old Sith Empires collapsed because rival Sith Lords constantly betrayed each other, diluting their strength. [film_pt] Darth Sidious, born Sheev Palpatine, was trained by Darth Plagueis, a Sith Lord obsessed with manipulating midi-chlorians to cheat death. Sidious murdered Plagueis in his sleep and took Darth Maul as his first apprentice. [film_pt] After Maul's apparent defeat on Naboo, Sidious recruited the fallen Jedi Count Dooku, who became Darth Tyranus and served as the public leader of the Separatist movement. [film_pt] Sidious orchestrated the entire Clone Wars as a grand manipulation — controlling both sides — to destroy the Jedi and transform the Republic into the Galactic Empire. He seduced Anakin Skywalker to the dark side by promising the power to prevent death, christening him Darth Vader. [film_pt]

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]

Darth Maul survived his bisection on Naboo, driven mad by pain and rage, and was eventually restored by his brother Savage Opress. [tv_tcw] Maul built a criminal empire known as the Shadow Collective, uniting Death Watch, the Black Sun, and the Pyke Syndicate under his rule. He briefly conquered Mandalore before Sidious personally intervened, defeating Maul in combat and killing Savage. [tv_tcw] Sidious spared Maul, intending to use him as a pawn — demonstrating the Sith principle that even a former apprentice is merely a tool. [tv_tcw] Count Dooku, as Darth Tyranus, secretly trained dark-side assassins like Asajj Ventress, though he discarded her when Sidious deemed her a threat to the Rule of Two. [tv_tcw]

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]

After the rise of the Empire, Vader served as Sidious's enforcer for two decades, hunting Jedi survivors and crushing dissent. [film_ot] The Inquisitors operated under Vader's command but were not true Sith — the Rule of Two was maintained strictly between Sidious and Vader. [tv_rebels] Maul, still alive, sought ancient Sith and Jedi knowledge on Malachor, where he encountered Ezra Bridger and attempted to corrupt the young Jedi before eventually meeting his end at the hands of Obi-Wan Kenobi on Tatooine. [tv_rebels]

[spoiler_tier: sequel_trilogy]

The Sith as a formal lineage appeared to end with the deaths of Sidious and Vader at the Battle of Endor. [film_st] However, Sidious had cheated death through dark-side science and the secrets of Plagueis, transferring his consciousness to a cloned body on the hidden Sith world of Exegol. [film_st] From Exegol he created Snoke as a puppet leader for the First Order and built the Final Order — a massive fleet of Star Destroyers each armed with planet-killing weapons. [film_st] Sidious was ultimately destroyed for good by Rey, who channeled the strength of all past Jedi to deflect his own Force lightning back upon him. [film_st]

[spoiler_tier: everything]

In the ancient era, the Sith were originally a species on Korriban, enslaved and interbred with by exiled dark Jedi. [legends] Darth Bane's full history details his rise from a cortosis miner to the sole surviving Sith Lord, who studied the holocron of Darth Revan to formulate the Rule of Two. [legends] Darth Revan, a Jedi Knight who fell to the dark side during the Mandalorian Wars, became one of the most powerful Sith Lords in history before being redeemed and returning to the light — his dual legacy as both Jedi and Sith made him a uniquely influential figure across millennia of Force tradition. [legends]
