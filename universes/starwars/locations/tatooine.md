# Tatooine

## Overview

Tatooine is a harsh desert world in the Outer Rim Territories, orbiting twin suns in a system largely ignored by galactic civilization. Controlled by the Hutts — particularly Jabba Desilijic Tiure — it serves as a haven for smugglers, bounty hunters, and those who prefer to avoid the attention of any central government. Despite its apparent insignificance, Tatooine has been the starting point for the galaxy's most consequential figures.

## Key Facts

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
- **Geography:** Endless dunes, rocky canyons, and scattered moisture farms. Water is the most valuable commodity. The twin suns — Tatoo I and Tatoo II — make the surface brutally hot during the day. [film_ot]
- **Inhabitants:** Jawas (small, hooded scavengers who trade in salvaged droids and machinery), Tusken Raiders (Sand People, indigenous nomads fiercely territorial over their lands), and scattered human colonists running moisture farms. [film_ot]
- **Mos Eisley:** The largest spaceport on the planet. A "wretched hive of scum and villainy," as Obi-Wan Kenobi described it. Where Luke Skywalker and Obi-Wan first met Han Solo and Chewbacca in Docking Bay 94. [film_ot]
- **Luke Skywalker's childhood:** Raised by Owen and Beru Lars on a moisture farm outside Anchorhead. Spent his youth dreaming of leaving, never knowing his father was Darth Vader or that Obi-Wan Kenobi watched over him from the Jundland Wastes. [film_ot]
- **Jabba's Palace:** Fortress of Jabba the Hutt, located in the Northern Dune Sea. Han Solo was held here frozen in carbonite. Luke, Leia, Lando, and Chewbacca mounted a rescue operation that ended with Jabba's death aboard his sail barge over the Great Pit of Carkoon. [film_ot]

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
- **Anakin Skywalker's birthplace:** Born into slavery under the junk dealer Watto. His mother Shmi claimed there was no father — he was conceived by the Force itself. Discovered by Qui-Gon Jinn during a stop for ship repairs. Won his freedom through a podrace but had to leave his mother behind. [film_pt]
- **Shmi's death:** Anakin returned years later as a Jedi Padawan, too late. Shmi had been captured by Tusken Raiders. He found her dying in their camp and slaughtered the entire village — men, women, and children. The first step on the path to the dark side. [film_pt]

[spoiler_tier: new_republic_era]
- **Post-Empire:** After the fall of the Empire, Tatooine remained largely lawless. Boba Fett survived the sarlacc pit and eventually claimed Jabba's former territory, attempting to rule Mos Espa as a more honorable crime lord. Din Djarin passed through multiple times during his travels with Grogu. [tv_mando]

[spoiler_tier: sequel_trilogy]
- **Rey's pilgrimage:** Rey visited the Lars homestead and buried Luke and Leia's lightsabers in the sand, honoring their legacy. When asked her name, she chose "Skywalker." The twin suns set on the saga where it began. [film_st]
