# Grogu

[spoiler_tier: new_republic_era]
## Overview
Grogu, initially known only as "The Child," is a Force-sensitive infant of the same rare and unnamed species as Jedi Grand Master Yoda. [tv_mando] Despite being approximately fifty years old, he is developmentally equivalent to a toddler — his species ages extraordinarily slowly. He has large green ears, wide dark eyes, and an insatiable curiosity. He communicates through coos, gestures, and the Force.

## Origins
Grogu was raised in the Jedi Temple on Coruscant during the Republic era. [tv_mando] He survived Order 66 — someone smuggled him out of the Temple during the purge. The details of his rescue remain unclear. He was hidden for decades, eventually falling into the hands of an Imperial remnant led by Moff Gideon, who sought his midi-chlorian-rich blood for unknown experiments potentially related to cloning Force-sensitive beings.

## The Force
Grogu is naturally strong in the Force, capable of feats that belie his size and apparent age. [tv_mando] He has demonstrated telekinesis, Force healing, and the ability to choke enemies — a dark side technique that echoes his unknown training history. He instinctively uses the Force to protect Din Djarin, though the effort often exhausts him to the point of unconsciousness. His connection to the Force is raw and emotional, unrefined by formal discipline.

## Rescued by the Mandalorian
Din Djarin was contracted to deliver Grogu to an Imperial client but rescued him instead, becoming his protector. [tv_mando] Their bond deepened through months of shared danger. Ahsoka Tano was the first to learn Grogu's name through the Force and revealed his history at the Jedi Temple. She refused to train him, sensing his powerful attachment to Din — and the fear that attachment carried.

## Choice
Luke Skywalker answered Grogu's call from the seeing stone on Tython. [tv_mando] Luke offered to train Grogu in the ways of the Jedi, presenting him with a choice: Yoda's lightsaber or Din's beskar chain mail. Grogu chose the armor — chose his father. He returned to Din, abandoning formal Jedi training for the path of a Mandalorian foundling. He was officially adopted as Din Grogu.

## Significance
Grogu is a living thread connecting the prequel era, the original trilogy era, and the New Republic era. [tv_mando] Raised in the Jedi Temple, survivor of Order 66, hidden through the Empire's reign, and adopted by a Mandalorian — his life is a microcosm of the galaxy's upheavals. His choice of attachment over detachment inverts the Jedi teaching that destroyed Anakin Skywalker, suggesting a different way forward.
