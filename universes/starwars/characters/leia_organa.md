# Leia Organa

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
Princess Leia Organa of Alderaan is a leader of the Rebel Alliance and one of the most important figures in galactic history. [film_ot] A senator at a young age, she secretly funneled intelligence and resources to the Rebellion. She was captured by Darth Vader while transporting the stolen Death Star plans, but even under interrogation and the threat of Alderaan's destruction, she did not break. She was rescued by Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, and Chewbacca aboard the Death Star.

## The Rebellion
Leia served as a key strategist and field leader for the Rebel Alliance. [film_ot] She organized the evacuation of Hoth, endured capture on Bespin, led the rescue of Han Solo from Jabba the Hutt, and commanded the ground team on Endor. Her relationship with Han Solo deepened through the war — from mutual antagonism to love. She discovered that Luke Skywalker was her twin brother, a revelation that connected her to the Force.

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
## True Origins
Leia is the daughter of Anakin Skywalker and Padme Amidala, born alongside her twin Luke on Polis Massa. [film_pt] After Padme's death, Senator Bail Organa of Alderaan adopted Leia and raised her as his own daughter. She was hidden from the Empire — and from Darth Vader, her biological father. Her Force sensitivity was present from birth but remained largely untrained for decades.

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]
## The Destruction of Alderaan
Grand Moff Tarkin destroyed Alderaan as a demonstration of the Death Star's power, killing billions — including Bail Organa. [film_ot] Leia witnessed the destruction of her homeworld. This loss fueled her resolve but never turned her toward vengeance. She remained a diplomat and strategist first, a warrior when necessary.

[spoiler_tier: new_republic_era]
## The New Republic Era
After the fall of the Empire, Leia served in the New Republic Senate and had a son, Ben Solo, with Han. [film_st] She sensed darkness growing in Ben but was unable to prevent his turn. When the political establishment proved unwilling to confront the rising First Order, Leia founded the Resistance — a small, underfunded military organization operating outside official channels.

[spoiler_tier: sequel_trilogy]
## General Organa
As General Organa, Leia led the Resistance against the First Order. [film_st] She was nearly killed when the bridge of her flagship was destroyed but survived by instinctively using the Force to pull herself to safety. She carried the burden of knowing her son had become Kylo Ren and had killed Han. She trained Rey in the ways of the Force, having herself trained as a Jedi under Luke years earlier — she had chosen to set that path aside, sensing it would lead to her son's death.

## Her Final Act
Leia reached out to Ben through the Force during his duel with Rey, calling to him one last time. [film_st] The effort cost her life. Her sacrifice, combined with Han's memory, turned Ben Solo back to the light. Leia became one with the Force, disappearing at the same moment as Ben after his redemption.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Legacy
Leia Organa's Force ghost appeared alongside Luke's on Tatooine. [film_st] She was a princess, senator, general, Jedi, and mother — each role shaped by loss and sustained by an unbreakable will. The galaxy remembered her as the heart of every resistance against tyranny.
