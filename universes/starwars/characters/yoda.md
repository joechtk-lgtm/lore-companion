# Yoda

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
Yoda is a Jedi Master living in exile on the swamp planet Dagobah. [film_ot] Small, ancient, and deceptively powerful, he trained Luke Skywalker in the ways of the Force after Obi-Wan Kenobi's death. He is approximately 900 years old at the time of the original trilogy. His species has no known name. He speaks in inverted syntax — a habit that masks one of the sharpest minds in galactic history.

## Training Luke
Obi-Wan's Force ghost directed Luke to Dagobah to complete his training. [film_ot] Yoda initially tested Luke by disguising his true nature, appearing as a foolish swamp creature. He trained Luke in telekinesis, meditation, and the philosophy of the Force. He warned Luke against confronting Vader before his training was complete and cautioned him about the dark side's seductive pull.

## Death
Yoda died of old age on Dagobah, shortly before the Battle of Endor. [film_ot] His final words to Luke confirmed that Vader was indeed his father and revealed there was "another Skywalker" — Leia. He became one with the Force, his body vanishing. He appeared as a Force ghost at the celebration on Endor.

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
## Grand Master of the Jedi Order
Yoda served as Grand Master of the Jedi Order for centuries, training generations of younglings. [film_pt] He was the most respected voice on the Jedi Council. He sensed the darkness surrounding Anakin Skywalker from the beginning, warning against training the boy, but ultimately deferred to the Council's decision. He led clone troopers in the Battle of Geonosis and served as a general in the Clone Wars.

## Failure and Exile
Yoda failed to defeat Darth Sidious in their confrontation in the Senate chamber. [film_pt] This duel — fought among the floating platforms of the Galactic Senate — ended in a draw, but Yoda was forced to retreat. He recognized that the Jedi had lost. He went into exile on Dagobah, a planet strong in the dark side that would mask his presence. He meditated on the failures of the Jedi Order for two decades.

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]
## The Clone Wars and the Force
During the Clone Wars, Yoda undertook a spiritual journey guided by the voice of Qui-Gon Jinn's Force ghost. [tv_tcw] He traveled to the wellspring of the Force, confronted manifestations of his own darkness, and learned the secrets of preserving consciousness after death. He was among the first to understand that the war itself was the Sith's weapon — not just one battle but the entire conflict was designed to destroy the Jedi.

[spoiler_tier: sequel_trilogy]
## Force Ghost
Yoda appeared as a Force ghost to Luke on Ahch-To, summoning lightning to burn the tree containing the ancient Jedi texts. [film_st] He reminded Luke that failure is the greatest teacher, and that the true burden of a master is to be surpassed by the student. His humor and wisdom remained undimmed in death.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Legacy
Yoda's voice was among the Jedi who spoke to Rey during the Battle of Exegol. [film_st] He trained Jedi for over 800 years, yet the Order fell on his watch. His greatest wisdom came not from his centuries of teaching but from his years of reflection in exile — the understanding that the Jedi had become blind to their own failures.
