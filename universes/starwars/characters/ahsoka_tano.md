# Ahsoka Tano

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta Force user who was once a Jedi but left the Order before its fall. [tv_tcw] She is not a Sith, not a Jedi — something else entirely. She wields two white lightsabers, purified from red Inquisitor kyber crystals. Her story spans the Clone Wars, the Rebellion, and beyond.

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]
## Anakin's Padawan
Ahsoka was assigned as Padawan to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars — a pairing neither expected. [tv_tcw] She was young, headstrong, and eager. Anakin's unorthodox teaching style suited her perfectly. She fought in major campaigns including the Battle of Christophsis, the rescue of Jabba's son, the defense of Onderon, and the siege of Mandalore. She grew from an impulsive student into a capable warrior and leader.

## The Trial and Departure
Ahsoka was framed for bombing the Jedi Temple hangar. [tv_tcw] The Jedi Council expelled her and turned her over to a Republic military tribunal. Anakin proved her innocence by unmasking the true bomber — Barriss Offee, a fellow Padawan who had lost faith in the Jedi. The Council invited Ahsoka back, calling her expulsion "her great trial." She refused. She walked away from the Order, from Anakin, and from the only life she had known. It was the right decision — and it saved her life when Order 66 came.

## Surviving Order 66
Ahsoka was with clone commander Rex during Order 66. [tv_tcw] Rex resisted the command long enough to warn her. She removed his inhibitor chip, and together they escaped. She faked her death, leaving her lightsabers behind on a crashed ship. She disappeared into the galaxy as the Empire rose.

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]
## Fulcrum
Under the codename "Fulcrum," Ahsoka served as an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance, coordinating cells and gathering information. [tv_rebels] She worked alongside Bail Organa and eventually revealed herself to the crew of the Ghost — Hera Syndulla, Kanan Jarrus, and Ezra Bridger. She became a mentor figure, drawing on her experience while carrying the weight of the Jedi Order's failure.

## Confrontation with Vader
On Malachor, Ahsoka confronted Darth Vader. [tv_rebels] She refused to believe Anakin could have become this — until she slashed his mask open and saw his eye. "Anakin," she said. "Anakin Skywalker was weak. I destroyed him," Vader replied. She declared she would not leave him — not this time. Their duel ended ambiguously. She survived, pulled through time by Ezra Bridger using the World Between Worlds — a mystical plane within the Force.

[spoiler_tier: new_republic_era]
## After the Empire
In the New Republic era, Ahsoka searched for Grand Admiral Thrawn, who had disappeared along with Ezra Bridger. [tv_mando] She encountered Din Djarin and Grogu, and was the first to communicate with the child, learning his name and history through the Force. She refused to train Grogu, sensing his strong attachment to Din — an echo of the attachments that destroyed Anakin. She directed Din to the Jedi temple on Tython instead.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## The Continuing Search
Ahsoka's journey continued with her quest to find Ezra Bridger and confront the threat of Thrawn's return. [tv_mando] She is one of the few living beings who knew Anakin Skywalker as he truly was — before the fall. She carries that knowledge like a scar. She is neither Jedi nor Sith, operating by her own code: compassion without attachment, strength without domination.
