# Obi-Wan Kenobi

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
Obi-Wan Kenobi is a Jedi Master who lived as a hermit on Tatooine under the name "Ben Kenobi." [film_ot] He watched over young Luke Skywalker from a distance for nearly two decades. When Luke encountered R2-D2's message from Princess Leia, Obi-Wan revealed Luke's heritage — that his father had been a Jedi Knight — and gave him Anakin's lightsaber. He began Luke's training in the Force.

## Sacrifice on the Death Star
Aboard the Death Star, Obi-Wan confronted Darth Vader — a foe he knew well. [film_ot] He allowed Vader to strike him down, becoming one with the Force. His voice continued to guide Luke: "Use the Force, Luke" during the trench run, and he appeared as a Force ghost on Dagobah and at Endor. He told Luke that Vader had "killed" his father — a truth "from a certain point of view."

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
## Qui-Gon's Apprentice
Obi-Wan was the Padawan of Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn. [film_pt] During the Naboo crisis, he witnessed Qui-Gon's discovery of Anakin Skywalker and the Jedi Council's refusal to train the boy. When Darth Maul killed Qui-Gon, Obi-Wan defeated the Sith Lord in combat — the first Jedi to kill a Sith in a millennium. He was granted the rank of Jedi Knight and honored Qui-Gon's dying wish to train Anakin.

## Master and Apprentice
Obi-Wan trained Anakin Skywalker from boyhood to knighthood. [film_pt] Their relationship was deep but complicated — part brotherhood, part mentorship, strained by Anakin's emotions and the Council's demands. Obi-Wan uncovered the clone army on Kamino and fought in the Battle of Geonosis, the first engagement of the Clone Wars. He became a Jedi Master and member of the Jedi Council.

## The Duel on Mustafar
After Anakin's fall to the dark side and the execution of Order 66, Obi-Wan confronted his former apprentice on Mustafar. [film_pt] He defeated Anakin, severing his limbs and leaving him burning on the volcanic shore. He could not bring himself to deliver the killing blow. He took Anakin's lightsaber and delivered the newborn Luke to Owen and Beru Lars on Tatooine. This was the beginning of his exile.

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]
## The Clone Wars
Obi-Wan served as a Jedi General throughout the Clone Wars, often alongside Anakin and Ahsoka Tano. [tv_tcw] He was known for his measured approach, preferring negotiation to violence — earning the title "The Negotiator." He had a complicated relationship with Duchess Satine Kryze of Mandalore, a love he set aside to honor the Jedi Code. He faced Darth Maul again after the Sith survived their first encounter, and was devastated when Maul killed Satine.

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]
## Exile on Tatooine
For nineteen years, Obi-Wan lived in isolation in the Jundland Wastes. [film_ot] He watched Luke grow up from a distance, honoring his promise to protect the boy. He communed with the Force ghost of Qui-Gon Jinn, learning the technique of preserving consciousness after death — a skill he would later use himself.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Legacy
Obi-Wan's voice was among the Jedi who called to Rey during the Battle of Exegol. [film_st] He remains one of the most respected Jedi in galactic memory — a man who held to the Code even when it cost him everything. His greatest failure was Anakin's fall; his greatest triumph was that Anakin's son redeemed what Obi-Wan could not.
