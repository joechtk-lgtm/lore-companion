# Palpatine

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
The Emperor is the ruler of the Galactic Empire — a tyrant who dissolved the Senate and governs through fear, the Imperial military, and the dark side of the Force. [film_ot] He is a Sith Lord, the master of Darth Vader. He resides aboard the second Death Star during the Battle of Endor, where he attempts to turn Luke Skywalker to the dark side. When Luke refuses, the Emperor attacks him with Force lightning. Vader intervenes, killing the Emperor by throwing him into the reactor shaft.

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
## Senator and Chancellor
Palpatine was Senator of Naboo, a seemingly mild-mannered politician. [film_pt] He maneuvered the Trade Federation's invasion of his own planet to engineer a vote of no confidence in Chancellor Valorum, positioning himself for election as Supreme Chancellor. He was Darth Sidious — a Sith Lord who had trained under Darth Plagueis, whom he later killed in his sleep.

## The Grand Plan
Palpatine orchestrated both sides of the Clone Wars. [film_pt] He commissioned the clone army through Sith intermediaries while simultaneously controlling the Separatist movement through Count Dooku (Darth Tyranus). The war was a machine designed to centralize power in his hands, weaken the Jedi, and justify emergency authority. He accumulated absolute power while the Republic applauded.

## Revelation and Order 66
Palpatine revealed his Sith identity to Anakin Skywalker, tempting him with the power to save Padme from death. [film_pt] When Mace Windu attempted to arrest him, Palpatine used the confrontation to turn Anakin — claiming the Jedi were attempting a coup. He issued Order 66, activating the clone troopers' inhibitor chips and triggering the galaxy-wide massacre of the Jedi. He declared himself Emperor and reorganized the Republic into the Galactic Empire. "So this is how liberty dies — with thunderous applause."

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]
## Manipulating the War
Throughout the Clone Wars, Sidious manipulated events from the shadows. [tv_tcw] He ordered Dooku to train assassins, orchestrated the siege of Mandalore, and personally intervened when Darth Maul threatened his plans. He ensured the inhibitor chips in the clones remained secret, silencing anyone — including clone trooper Fives — who came close to uncovering the truth.

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]
## The Emperor's Rule
Under Palpatine's Empire, the galaxy was governed through military occupation, fear, and the suppression of all dissent. [film_ot] The construction of the Death Star was his ultimate expression of power — the Tarkin Doctrine made manifest. He foresaw nearly every threat to his rule, except the love between a father and son.

[spoiler_tier: sequel_trilogy]
## Return from Death
Palpatine survived his apparent death at Endor through dark side techniques, transferring his consciousness to clone bodies on the hidden Sith world of Exegol. [film_st] He spent decades building the Final Order — a fleet of planet-killing Star Destroyers. He created Snoke as a puppet to corrupt Ben Solo and manipulate the First Order. He was Rey's grandfather, having fathered a son who fled Exegol. Palpatine sought Rey as a vessel for his spirit but was destroyed when she channeled the power of all past Jedi against him.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## The Sith Eternal
Palpatine's cult, the Sith Eternal, worshipped him on Exegol for decades. [film_st] His contingency plans — Operation Cinder, the First Order, the Final Order — reveal a mind that planned for his own death. Whether his final destruction at Exegol truly ended the Sith lineage remains the defining question of the saga.
