# Revan

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
Revan is a figure from the ancient history of the galaxy, thousands of years before the rise of the Empire. [legends] **Note: Revan is a Legends (non-canon) character. All information here comes from the Star Wars Legends continuity, primarily the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic (2003) and related material. Revan is not part of the current official Star Wars canon.**

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
## The Mandalorian Wars
Revan was a Jedi Knight during the era of the Old Republic. [legends] When the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders launched a devastating war of conquest against the Republic, the Jedi Council refused to intervene. Revan defied the Council, rallying a faction of Jedi to join the Republic military. Alongside Malak, his closest ally, Revan became the Republic's greatest general. He was brilliant, charismatic, and ruthless in his tactics. He defeated the Mandalorians at the Battle of Malachor V using a superweapon — the Mass Shadow Generator — that destroyed the planet and ended the war at a terrible cost.

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]
## Fall to the Dark Side
After the Mandalorian Wars, Revan and Malak ventured into the Unknown Regions, where they discovered the Sith Empire and its Emperor. [legends] They were corrupted — or chose to be corrupted — by the dark side. They returned to the Republic as Sith Lords: Darth Revan and Darth Malak. Revan built a Sith armada using the Star Forge, an ancient Rakatan space station that drew on the dark side to produce unlimited warships. The Jedi Civil War began — Republic against Republic, Jedi against former Jedi.

## Betrayal and Capture
Darth Malak betrayed Revan, firing on his master's flagship during a Jedi strike team's boarding action. [legends] Revan was critically wounded. The Jedi Bastila Shan captured the broken Sith Lord and, rather than letting him die, used the Force to save his life. The Jedi Council erased Revan's memories and implanted a new identity — a common Republic soldier with no knowledge of his past.

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]
## Redemption
Revan, unaware of his true identity, was stationed aboard the Republic ship Endar Spire when Malak's forces attacked. [legends] He escaped with Carth Onasi and encountered Bastila Shan on Taris. Together they searched for Star Maps — ancient Rakatan artifacts that revealed the location of the Star Forge. Revan's Force abilities returned gradually. He gathered companions: the Wookiee Zaalbar, the Twi'lek Mission Vao, the Mandalorian Canderous Ordo, the droid HK-47 (whom Revan himself had originally built), and others.

On the Leviathan, Malak revealed the truth: the player character was Revan. The memories returned. Revan faced a choice: reclaim the mantle of Sith Lord, or fight against everything he had once built. In the canonical Legends telling, Revan chose the light. He destroyed the Star Forge and defeated Darth Malak, ending the Jedi Civil War.

[spoiler_tier: new_republic_era]
## After the Star Forge
Revan's memories continued to surface after the war. [legends] He remembered the Sith Emperor in the Unknown Regions — the true threat that had originally drawn him and Malak beyond known space. He left the Republic, traveling alone into the Unknown Regions to confront this ancient evil. He was captured by the Sith Emperor and imprisoned for three hundred years, his mind used as a buffer against the Emperor's madness.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## Legacy
Revan's legacy is one of extremes: savior and destroyer, Jedi and Sith, redeemed and imprisoned. [legends] He is one of the most debated figures in Legends lore — a character who embodies the idea that the Force is not a simple binary of light and dark. The Revanite cult persisted for centuries after his disappearance, worshipping his philosophy that one could master both sides of the Force. Whether this is wisdom or hubris remains the central question of his story. His mask — half Mandalorian, half shadow — became an icon.
