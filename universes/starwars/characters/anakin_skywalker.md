# Anakin Skywalker

[spoiler_tier: original_trilogy]
## Overview
Anakin Skywalker is a legendary figure in the history of the Jedi Order and the galaxy at large. [film_ot] He was once a Jedi Knight — a hero of the Republic before a darker chapter. His name carries weight among those who remember the Clone Wars. Obi-Wan Kenobi described him as "a good friend" and "the best star-pilot in the galaxy." Luke Skywalker is his son.

The truth of Anakin's fate — that he became Darth Vader — is revealed by Vader himself aboard Cloud City. This is the central revelation of the original trilogy. [film_ot]

[spoiler_tier: prequel_trilogy]
## Discovery on Tatooine
Anakin was born a slave on Tatooine, raised by his mother Shmi Skywalker. [film_pt] He had no father — his conception was seemingly linked to the Force itself. Qui-Gon Jinn discovered him during the Naboo crisis and believed him to be the Chosen One of Jedi prophecy, destined to bring balance to the Force. His midi-chlorian count was the highest ever recorded. Anakin won the Boonta Eve Classic podrace — remarkable for a human child — earning his freedom. He left Tatooine, leaving his mother behind in slavery.

## Jedi Training and Forbidden Love
The Jedi Council was reluctant to train Anakin, sensing fear and attachment in him. [film_pt] After Qui-Gon's death at the hands of Darth Maul, Obi-Wan Kenobi took Anakin as his Padawan. Anakin grew into a powerful but impulsive Jedi. He secretly married Senator Padme Amidala on Naboo — a direct violation of the Jedi Code forbidding attachment.

## The Fall
Anakin's mother was captured by Tusken Raiders on Tatooine. He arrived too late to save her and slaughtered the entire camp — men, women, and children. [film_pt] This was a harbinger of his fall. Later, plagued by visions of Padme dying in childbirth, Anakin was manipulated by Chancellor Palpatine — secretly Darth Sidious — who promised the power to cheat death. Anakin betrayed Mace Windu, pledged himself to Sidious, and became Darth Vader. He led the assault on the Jedi Temple during Order 66. On Mustafar, Obi-Wan defeated him in combat, leaving him burned and broken. He was sealed in the black life-support suit.

[spoiler_tier: clone_wars_era]
## The Clone Wars
During the Clone Wars, Anakin served as a Jedi General and was one of the Republic's greatest warriors. [tv_tcw] He was bold, unorthodox, and fiercely loyal. He took Ahsoka Tano as his Padawan — a relationship that deeply shaped both of them. He led campaigns on Christophsis, Ryloth, and countless other worlds. His bond with Obi-Wan was legendary; they were called "The Team" by Republic forces. His growing darkness was visible in moments of ruthlessness — his torture of Poggle the Lesser, his fury when those he loved were threatened.

[spoiler_tier: rebellion_era]
## Redemption and Legacy
At the Battle of Endor, Darth Vader turned against the Emperor to save his son Luke, hurling Palpatine into the reactor shaft. [film_ot] In doing so, he fulfilled the prophecy of the Chosen One — destroying the Sith and restoring balance to the Force. Anakin Skywalker died free, appearing as a Force ghost alongside Obi-Wan and Yoda.

[spoiler_tier: sequel_trilogy]
## Echo in the Sequels
Anakin's grandson, Ben Solo, idolized Darth Vader and sought to finish what Vader started. [film_st] Vader's burned helmet became an object of dark reverence for Kylo Ren. During the Battle of Exegol, Rey heard Anakin's voice among the chorus of past Jedi encouraging her to rise — confirming his enduring presence in the Force.

[spoiler_tier: everything]
## The Chosen One Debate
Whether Anakin truly fulfilled the prophecy remains debated by scholars of the Force. [film_pt] [film_ot] He destroyed the Sith at Endor, but the dark side endured through Palpatine's return. Some interpret balance as the destruction of both Sith and the old Jedi Order — a reading supported by the collapse of both institutions during Anakin's lifetime.
