# Spoiler Tiers: Star Wars

## original_trilogy
Episodes IV, V, VI only. A New Hope, The Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi. Includes: Luke's journey from Tatooine farm boy to Jedi Knight. Han Solo and the Rebellion. Leia as rebel leader. Darth Vader as the primary antagonist. The Emperor as the dark power behind the throne. The destruction of the Death Star. Vader's reveal as Luke's father. Leia revealed as Luke's sister. Vader's redemption and the Emperor's defeat at Endor.

## prequel_trilogy
All six saga films. Adds: Anakin Skywalker's full origin story. His discovery on Tatooine, training under Obi-Wan Kenobi, forbidden marriage to Padmé Amidala. Palpatine's identity as Darth Sidious. The Clone Wars as engineered conflict. Anakin's fall to the dark side and transformation into Darth Vader. The destruction of the Jedi Order. The birth of Luke and Leia. Obi-Wan's exile to Tatooine. Yoda's exile to Dagobah.

## clone_wars_era
Films + The Clone Wars animated series complete. Adds: Ahsoka Tano — Anakin's Padawan, her departure from the Jedi Order. The full scope of Order 66 and its clones' perspective. Maul's survival and crime syndicate. Mandalore's fall during the Siege. Rex and the clones as individuals. The political corruption of the Republic. Ventress, Savage Opress, and the Nightsisters. The deeper tragedy of the Jedi's blindness.

## rebellion_era
Rebels, Andor, Rogue One added. The formation of the Rebel Alliance. Cassian Andor's radicalization. Kanan Jarrus and Ezra Bridger — Jedi survivors. The World Between Worlds. Ahsoka's survival confirmed. Grand Admiral Thrawn's role. The theft of the Death Star plans. Jyn Erso's sacrifice on Scarif. The Inquisitors hunting surviving Jedi.

## new_republic_era
The Mandalorian, Ahsoka series added. Din Djarin and Grogu's story. The fall of the Imperial remnants. Moff Gideon's pursuit of Grogu. The Darksaber and Mandalorian succession. Luke's early attempts at a new Jedi academy. Ahsoka's search for Ezra Bridger. Thrawn's return from exile.

## sequel_trilogy
All nine saga films. The First Order's rise from Imperial ashes. Snoke as Supreme Leader. Kylo Ren — Ben Solo's fall and his relationship with Rey. Rey's identity as Palpatine's granddaughter. Palpatine's survival and return. Luke's self-imposed exile after Ben's turn. Han Solo's death at his son's hand. Luke's final stand on Crait. Leia's death reaching out to Ben. Ben Solo's redemption. Rey taking the Skywalker name.

## everything
All canon sources without restriction. Every series, film, novel, and comic in current Disney canon. All Legends content if those sources are enabled. No spoiler protection.
